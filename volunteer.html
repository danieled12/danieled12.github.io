Volunteer Poster

Objective:

Create a poster that have an inviting feel for people to volunteer.

Audience:

Students or workers looking to volunteer.

Software Used:

Adobe Photoshop

Techniques Used:

Creating the background effect and adding different effects to it. Creating the hand design and making it have a spiral effect to draw the audiences eyes in. Adding and creating effects on the text and using a hierarchy to create a visually appealing poster.
